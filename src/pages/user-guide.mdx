## Time Controls
### Timescale increment/decrement:
To increment or decrement the timescale by one, use the arrows to either side of the slider.

Alternatively, the left/right <KBD><ArrowLeft/></KBD> <KBD><ArrowRight/></KBD> arrow keys, or the <KBD>4</KBD> and <KBD>6</KBD> keys on the numpad.


### Timescale units:
To cycle through the timescale units (Seconds, Minutes, Hours, Days), click the unit on the time panel.  
![timescale-units](/images/timescale-units.png)  

Alternatively, <KBD>shift</KBD> + <KBD><ArrowLeft/></KBD> will decrease the units and <KBD>shift</KBD> + <KBD><ArrowRight/></KBD> will increase the units.  

### Sidereal Day Advancement:  
The buttons on either side of the Play/Pause button will advance time by +/- 1 sidereal Day.

Alternatively, you can use the up/down arrow keys <KBD><ArrowUp/></KBD> <KBD><ArrowDown/></KBD> or the <KBD>8</KBD> and <KBD>2</KBD> keys on the numpad.

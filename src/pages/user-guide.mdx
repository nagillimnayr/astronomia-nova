# User Guide


## Keyboard & Mouse Controls

## Time Controls
### Timescale increment/decrement:
- To increment or decrement the timescale by one, use the arrows to either side of the slider.

- Alternatively, the left/right <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-1'><KBD><ArrowLeft/></KBD> <KBD><ArrowRight/></KBD></span> arrow keys, or the <KBD>4</KBD> and <KBD>6</KBD> keys on the numpad.


### Timescale units:
- To cycle through the timescale units (Seconds, Minutes, Hours, Days), click the unit on the time panel.  
![timescale-units](/images/timescale-units.png)  

- Alternatively, <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-1'><KBD>shift</KBD> + <KBD><ArrowLeft/></KBD></span> will decrease the units and <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-1'><KBD>shift</KBD> + <KBD><ArrowRight/></KBD></span> will increase the units.  

### Sidereal Day Advancement:  
- The buttons on either side of the Play/Pause button will advance time by +/- 1 sidereal Day.

- Alternatively, you can use the up/down arrow keys <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center'> <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-1'><KBD><ArrowUp/></KBD> and <KBD><ArrowDown/></KBD></span> or the <span className='inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-1'><KBD>8</KBD> and <KBD>2</KBD></span></span> keys on the numpad.

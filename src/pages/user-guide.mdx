## Time Controls
### Timescale units:
To change the timescale units (Seconds, Minutes, Hours, Days), click the unit on the time panel.  
![](/images/timescale-units.png)  

Alternatively, the key <KBD>U</KBD> will also cycle through the units.  

### Sidereal Day Advancement:
The buttons on either side of the Play/Pause button will advance time by +/- 1 sidereal Day.

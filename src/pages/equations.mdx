### Universal Gravitation
$$ \displaystyle F_g = \frac{GMm}{r^2} $$

$$ \displaystyle a = \frac{GM}{r^2} = \frac{F_g}{m} $$

## Integration Methods

---

### Euler Method

$$ \displaystyle \vec{v}_{n+1} = \vec{v}_n + \vec{a}_n\Delta t $$  
$$ \displaystyle \vec{r}_{n+1} = \vec{r}_n + \vec{v}_n\Delta t $$

---

### Euler-Cromer Method 

$$ \displaystyle \vec{v}_{n+1} = \vec{v}_n + \vec{a}_n\Delta t $$  
$$ \displaystyle \vec{r}_{n+1} = \vec{r}_n + \vec{v}_{n+1}\Delta t $$

---

### Euler-Richardson Method

$$ \displaystyle \vec{a}_{n} = \frac{F\left(\vec{r}_{n}\right)}{m} $$  

$$ \displaystyle \vec{v}_{n+\frac{1}{2}} = \vec{v}_{n} + \frac{1}{2}\vec{a}_n\Delta t $$  

$$ \displaystyle \vec{r}_{n+\frac{1}{2}} = \vec{r}_{n} + \frac{1}{2}\vec{v}_{n}\Delta t $$

$$ \displaystyle \vec{a}_{n+\frac{1}{2}} = \frac{F\left( \vec{r}_{n+\frac{1}{2}}\right)}{m} $$ 


$$ \displaystyle \vec{v}_{n+1} = \vec{v}_n + \vec{a}_{\frac{1}{2}}\Delta t $$  

$$ \displaystyle \vec{r}_{n+1} = \vec{r}_n + \vec{v}_{\frac{1}{2}}\Delta t $$

---

## Velocity Verlet

$$ \displaystyle \vec{a}_{n} = \frac{F\left(\vec{r}_{n}\right)}{m} $$  

$$ \displaystyle \vec{r}_{n+1} = \vec{r}_{n} + \vec{v}_{n}\Delta t + \frac{1}{2}\vec{a}_{n}\Delta t^2 $$

$$ \displaystyle \vec{a}_{n+1} = \frac{F\left(\vec{r}_{n+1}\right)}{m} $$  

$$ \displaystyle \vec{v}_{n+1} = \vec{v}_{n} + \frac{1}{2}\left( \vec{a}_{n} + \vec{a}_{n+1} \right)\Delta t $$

### Vis-Viva

$$ \displaystyle |\vec{v}| = \sqrt{ GM \left( \frac{2}{r} - \frac{1}{a} \right) } $$

---
### Ascending Node

$$ \displaystyle \vec{n} = \vec{Z} \times \vec{h} $$

### Longitude of Ascending Node


$$ \displaystyle \Omega = \arccos{\left(\frac{\vec{n}\cdot\vec{X}}{n}  \right)} $$

---
### Argument of Periapsis


$$ \displaystyle \omega = \arccos{\left(\frac{\vec{n}\cdot\vec{e}}{ne}  \right)} $$

--- 
### True Anomaly

$$ \displaystyle \nu = \arccos{\left(\frac{\vec{r}\cdot\vec{e}}{re}  \right)} $$

---
### Semi-latus Rectum

$$ \displaystyle p = \frac{h^2}{\mu} $$

---
### Semi-major Axis

$$ \displaystyle a = \frac{p}{1 - e^2} $$

---
### Inclination

$$ \displaystyle i = \arccos{\left( \frac{ \vec{Z} \cdot \vec{h} }{h} \right)} $$
